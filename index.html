SB106

Summary:

The Pennsylvania General Assembly proposes amendments to the state constitution, addressing several issues: denying a constitutional right to taxpayer-funded abortions, modifying concurrent orders and resolutions, changing the Lieutenant Governor's selection process, updating elector qualifications including voter identification requirements, and mandating election audits conducted by the Auditor General or an Independent Auditor. These proposed amendments will be submitted as separate ballot questions at the first primary, general, or municipal election occurring at least three months after passing the General Assembly.
